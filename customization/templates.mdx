## Getting Started with Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Templates Page">
    Head to [haunt.gg/dashboard/templates](https://haunt.gg/dashboard/templates) to explore templates made by the community.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find One You Like">
    Use filters or search to browse templates by type or creation date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply It">
    Click **Use Template** to instantly apply it to your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What’s Inside the Templates Page

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="All Templates">
    - Public templates from all users  
    - Free and premium templates
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="My Templates">
    - Your own created templates  
    - Editable and removable anytime
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Favorites">
    - Templates you’ve starred for quick access
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Template Cards

<Steps>
  <Step title="Screenshot Preview">
    A preview image helps you see the template at a glance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tags & Info">
    Tags like `dark`, `minimal`, `y2k` show the style. Star count shows popularity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Quick Actions">
    Use it directly, preview, or share with a link.

    <Frame>
      <img src="../images/template-cards.png" alt="Use and Share Template" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Make Your Own Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create One">
    Click **Create Template** at the top-right.

    <Frame>
    <img src="../images/template-create.png" alt="Create Template" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out Details">
    Name it, add tags, upload an image, pick a privacy level, and hit **Create**.
    <Frame>
    <img src="../images/template-fillout.png" alt="Fillout Template" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share & Reuse">
    Once saved, your template appears in **My Templates**.  
    - **Public**: shows up in **All Templates** and is shareable  
    - **Private**: only visible to you  
    You can reuse or edit it anytime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit Your Template">
    Go to **My Templates** and click the **Edit** button:
    - Change the name
    - Update tags
    - Change Privacy Setting

    <Frame>
    <img src="../images/template-edit.png" alt="Edit Template" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete a Template">
    Click the **Delete** icon on a template card in **My Templates** to remove it permanently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Good to Know

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Limits">
    - Free users can save up to **3 templates**  
    - Premium users can save up to **5 templates**
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Tips">
    - Name templates clearly for easy searching  
    - Preview before applying  
    - Favorite the best ones for quicker access
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
