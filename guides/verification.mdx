## How to purchase a verified badge

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to the pricing page">
        Head over to [haunt.gg/pricing](https://haunt.gg/pricing)
    </Step>
    <Step title="Purchase a verified badge">
        Click on the **Purchase Verified Badge** button
    </Step>
    <Step title="Complete the verification process">
        Follow the instructions to complete the verification process
    </Step>
</Steps>

## How to apply for a verified badge

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="Dashboard">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Open Dashboard">
      Open your dashboard at [haunt.gg/dashboard](https://haunt.gg/dashboard).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Go to the Apply Section">
      Navigate to the [**Apply** section](https://haunt.gg/dashboard/apply).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Open Application Form">
      Select the [**Verified** application](https://haunt.gg/dashboard/apply/3) and begin filling it out.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Submit Your Application">
      Once you’ve completed the form, click **Submit Application**.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
  <Note>
    Please be patient after submitting your application — it may take some time to be reviewed.
  </Note>
</Tab>

    <Tab title="Discord">
        <Steps>
            <Step title="Join our Discord server">
                Head over to [discord.gg/hauntbio](https://discord.gg/hauntbio).
            </Step>
            <Step title="Go to the ticket channel">
                Go to the [ticket channel](https://discord.com/channels/1363890885443452989/1364576790185775155).
            </Step>
            <Step title="Create a ticket">
                Create a ticket and wait for a staff member to verify your account.
            </Step>
        </Steps>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Email">
        <Steps>
            <Step title="Ownership proof">
                Create a proof of ownership for your social media account.
            </Step>
            <Step title="Send an email">
                Send an email to [support@haunt.gg](mailto:support@haunt.gg) with your ownership proof & haunt.gg username.
            </Step>
        </Steps>
    </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Requirements

- A minimum of 10,000 followers/subscribers on any major platform.
- An authentic and active social media presence.
- Actively post content on your social media platforms.

<Note>
    Meeting the requirements does not guarantee verification. We will review your application and notify you of the outcome.
</Note>
